Frequently Asked Questions

1. How should I store Batter Breeze dosa batter?

Store it in the refrigerator at 4°C. Do not freeze the batter.

2. Is the batter salted? Can I adjust the salt?

Yes, the batter is lightly salted. You can adjust the salt to your taste.

3. Do I need to prepare the batter before cooking?

Simply remove the batter from the fridge 30 minutes before cooking for the best results.

4. Can I use Batter Breeze for both idlis and dosas?

Absolutely! Batter Breeze is versatile and perfect for making soft idlis and crispy dosas.
